Adventure Activities

Trekking

Explore the breathtaking landscapes of the Indian Himalayas on a trekking adventure. From the snow-capped peaks to lush green valleys, India offers a variety of trekking experiences for all levels of adventurers.

Helicopter Tours

Experience India from a bird's eye view with thrilling helicopter tours. Whether you want to soar over majestic mountains, vast deserts, or bustling cities, helicopter tours offer a unique perspective of India's diverse landscapes.

Hot Air Ballooning

Drift serenely above India's stunning scenery in a hot air balloon. From the vibrant colors of Rajasthan's desert to the lush greenery of Kerala's backwaters, hot air ballooning offers an unforgettable adventure.

Wildlife Safari

Embark on an exciting wildlife safari in India's national parks and reserves. Spot magnificent creatures like tigers, elephants, and rhinos in their natural habitats, and witness the incredible biodiversity of India's forests and grasslands.

Diving

Get lost in a world of calm and serenity filled with pristine beauty of the marine world with an unforgettable diving session in India. With a reputation of having few of the most beautiful coral formations in S.E. Asia, India has several diving spots around the country for an unforgettable diving tour.

Surfing

Experience the thrill of surfing along the picturesque coastlines of India. From the waves of Arugambay to the beaches of Tangalle and Trincomalee, India offers an exciting surfing experience for enthusiasts of all levels.
