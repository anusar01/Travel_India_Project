Taj Mahotsav - February 2025

Experience the vibrant cultural extravaganza at the Taj Mahotsav, held annually in February. Enjoy folk music, traditional dance performances, art exhibitions, and delicious cuisine from different parts of India.

Independence Day - August 15th, 2025

Celebrate India's Independence Day on August 15th with flag-hoisting ceremonies, patriotic songs, cultural programs, and parades across the country. It commemorates India's independence from British rule in 1947.

Holi Festival - March 2025

Join the colorful celebrations of Holi, the festival of colors, in March. Experience the joyous atmosphere as people smear each other with colors, dance to traditional music, and enjoy festive delicacies.

Vesak Festival - May 2025

Experience the spiritual significance of Vesak, celebrated on the full moon day in May. Join Buddhists across India in commemorating the birth, enlightenment, and passing away of Lord Buddha through prayer, meditation, and acts of kindness.

Rath Yatra - July 2025

Witness the grand procession of the Rath Yatra, an annual chariot festival celebrated in July. Join devotees as they pull the chariots of Lord Jagannath, Balabhadra, and Subhadra through the streets of Puri, Odisha.
